## Executive Summary

This analysis explores real estate sales performance using interactive Power BI dashboards. The data reveals strong overall revenue growth of **24.25%**, with **Apartments** leading in both sales volume and average revenue per transaction. However, a high churn rate of **42.69%** highlights significant customer retention opportunities.

## Key Insights

### 1. Revenue Trends
- Strong peak observed in **June 2022**.
- Revenue has been volatile since then.
- Overall growth stands at **+24.25%**.

### 2. Property Performance
- Apartments currently lead in both **sales volume** and **average revenue per transaction**.
- Houses, Land, and Commercial properties trail behind.
- Recommendation: Further analysis on margins and customer retention by property type.

### 3. Customer Behaviour & Churn
- High churn rate of **42.69%** is a critical area for improvement.
- **Target**: Reduce churn rate to **25%**.
- Churned customers’ revenue varies significantly across segments (Business Buyer, End-User, Investor, Renter-to-Owner).

### 4. Agent & Geographic Performance
- Agent **A00004** is the top performer.
- **Dhaka** dominates revenue share among cities.

## Recommendations

- Implement **targeted retention programs** and incentives for at-risk customer segments.
- Share best practices through **agent training** programs, leveraging insights from top performers.
- Run **seasonal campaigns** and optimize product mix to reduce revenue volatility.
- Focus marketing efforts on Apartments while improving offerings for other property types.
